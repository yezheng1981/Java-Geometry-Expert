Point by Point and Segment.


Click two points A and B, then click point C. Then click a point on a line or a circle to obtain a new point D on the line or the circle such that CD = AB.

Note that there are two solutions for point D if the distance from C to the line is less to AB. The JGEX prompts the user to choose which one.

D is the intersection of the circle (C, AB) and the line or circle.
